NYPD Shooting Incident - Data Analysis Project

In this project I analyzed a police incident dataset based in New York, USA, and communicated my findings. The goal with this analysis will give a comprehensive view of where a person will be safe if they were to move to New York City. The three main questions I asked were:1.What areas are the safest? 2. What segment of the population of New York City is most vulnerable to gun violence? 3. What time of day and year are most likely correlated with increased gun violence? This analysis would be very useful for those who are interested in moving or exploring new areas, but may be intimated due to being a minority and/or not knowing the area.
